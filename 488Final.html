Allison (Alli) is a student at Simmons School of Library and Information Science, advised by Laura Saunders and supported by an Allen Smith Endowed Scholarship. Her research is currently focused on developing a novel method of mapping negative space in academic publishing.

She is currently an Access Services Assistant at MIT Libraries, where she works with the circulation desk, course reserves, remote reference services, support for citation managment tools, and a myriad of other projects, teams, and committees. She also serves as Co-Chair of the Committee for the Promotion of Diversity and Inclusion.
